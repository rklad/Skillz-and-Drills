Why Us?

Skillz and Drills is dedicated to helping kids of all ages improve their game, whether it's baseball or softball, by providing a positive, supportive environment where young athletes can grow both on and off the field. Our experienced coaches are passionate about teaching the fundamentals, building confidence, and developing advanced skills through personalized instruction and engaging drills. We believe every player deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, and we tailor our programs to meet each child’s unique needs—regardless of their starting level. At Skillz and Drills, it’s not just about competition—it’s about growth, teamwork, and a lifelong love for the game.

Our Founder

Nick Scalzo (AKA Coach Scalzo) is the original founder of Skillz and Drills. He has coached high school baseball & softball for over 20 years now! He is currently the head coach for Marlboro High School's Softball Team and has been for the last 17 years. Under his leadership, the Mustangs went on to win back-to-back State Championships in 2023 and 2024. In 2023, Coach Scalzo was celebrated as NJ.com's Coach of the Year. The following year, he was Asbury Park Press's Coach of the Year. He has taught baseball and softball to children of all ages and will continue to do so as the founder of Skillz and Drills!
